Love is the prime directive of life, but we often shy away from applying love's equation into our day to day lives. This application is a vehicle to record one's experience in love, in all it's forms, from day to day. Learning to recognized and appreciate one's experience in love broadens one's capacity for love. When was the last time you experienced love? Make a record of it.
